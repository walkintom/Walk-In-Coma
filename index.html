Band

Walk In Coma are an alternative metal band who have been hitting the Essex live circuit for over a decade. Drawing from their combined influences of Slipknot, Lamb of God and Architects as well as many other eclectic individual tastes has given the band comparisons to Killswitch Engage, Machine Head and Bring Me The Horizon.

The Essex band formed in 2006 by school-mates Alex Adlington and Mark Elliott, with the latter's brother finishing the line up on drums. The trio started writing and gigging around their local area. Growing into their own sound and style and a few member adjustments, with Tom Case laying down the foundations of their wall of sound since 2010 and Joe Reid providing regimented, energy-charged drumming since 2014, the quartet have gone from strength to strength and are renowned for their energetic live shows. On the merit of this, Walk In Coma have been privileged to support Cancer Bats, Funeral for a Friend and Breed 77, as well as headlining numerous local shows.

After the success of 2015's "Narrenturm" the quartet are to release their latest 6-track EP "Blur the Line of Fiction and Fate" on 22nd June 2018. The music video for the EP's second track "Through Your Eyes" has been the band's most popular YouTube upload to date.
